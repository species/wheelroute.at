Currently, only the city center of Graz is mapped in a detailed way in OpenStreetMap. Want your area to be routable too? Add separate sidewalks, kerbs, and their incline an width to OSM!
The biggest part of the mapping was sponsored by In!tiativ.

Map data: OpenStreetMap, ODbL.
Routing: OSRM, BSD-2, for routing profiles see here.
